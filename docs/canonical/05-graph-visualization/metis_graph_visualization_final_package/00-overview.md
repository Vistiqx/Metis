# Graph Visualization Standards

## Purpose
The graph is the emotional and analytical center of Métis. These standards ensure every graph view feels premium, readable, and strategically useful.

## Design Thesis
A Métis graph should feel like:
- discovering structure in a signal field
- tracing intelligence through relationships
- navigating a living observatory of entities

## Core Principles
- shape communicates entity type
- color communicates state and meaning
- motion confirms discovery, not spectacle
- emphasis is sparse and intentional
- the graph must remain readable under density
