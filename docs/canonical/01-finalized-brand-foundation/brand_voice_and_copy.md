# Métis Brand Voice and Copy

## Core Voice

Métis should sound:

- authoritative
- precise
- analytical
- strategic
- discreet
- premium

Métis should not sound:

- playful
- casual
- trendy
- neon-cyberpunk
- hype-driven
- startup-generic

## Strategic Position

Métis is an elite intelligence analysis platform that reveals hidden patterns, relationships, and signals inside complex information environments.

## Messaging Pillars

### 1. Strategic Clarity
Métis transforms fragmented data into structured intelligence.

### 2. Relationship Discovery
Métis reveals hidden connections between people, organizations, sources, events, and digital activity.

### 3. Investigative Precision
Métis supports serious analyst workflows, not shallow search.

### 4. Executive Output
Métis helps turn investigation work into report-grade deliverables.

## Brand Promise

Métis reveals hidden relationships in complex intelligence environments.

## Homepage / Product Copy Style

Use copy that emphasizes:
- structured discovery
- confidence in findings
- strategic intelligence
- analyst-grade workflows
- evidence-backed reporting

## Good Product Language

Prefer:
- Investigations
- Entities
- Sources
- Findings
- Relationships
- Signals
- Reports
- Timeline
- Intelligence Activity

Avoid:
- playful microcopy
- slang
- generic AI hype phrases
- emojis
- consumer-app tone

## Report Copy Style

Reports should read as:
- evidence-based
- calm
- defensible
- professional
- direct

## Example Hero Messaging

### Hero
**Strategic Intelligence Through Structured Discovery**

### Supporting line
Reveal hidden relationships, investigate complex networks, and transform fragmented signals into decision-ready intelligence.

### CTA examples
- Request Demo
- Explore the Platform
- View Intelligence Workflows

## Copy Rule

When design and copy are paired, the tone must match the approved visual system:
- Wisdom Gold = authority / confirmed intelligence
- Signals = classification / findings
- dark environment = serious intelligence workspace
