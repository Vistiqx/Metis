
# Métis — Finalized Brand, UI, and Report System Summary

## 1. Brand Foundation

**Brand name:** Métis  
**Meaning:** Named after the Greek goddess of wisdom.  
**Core interpretation:** Métis is the source of wisdom and strategic intelligence, which is then revealed to and used by the end user.

### Final brand feeling
A hybrid of:

- Ancient Wisdom
- Strategic Instrument

The brand should feel like:

- elite intelligence platform
- strategic analysis instrument
- wisdom illuminating hidden patterns
- authoritative, precise, discreet, and premium

Not:

- generic AI startup
- neon cyberpunk toy
- playful SaaS dashboard
- gradient-heavy “AI” branding

---

# 2. Final Logo Direction

# 9. Report System

Two report modes.

## Digital Presentation Reports

Used for:

- executive delivery
- premium PDFs

Characteristics:

- dark backgrounds
- Wisdom Gold highlights
- constellation imagery

## Print-Friendly Reports

Used for:

- physical printing
- large report runs

Characteristics:

- white/light pages
- black typography
- minimal gold accents
- efficient charts

Rule:

Cover page → full color  
Interior pages → print efficient
