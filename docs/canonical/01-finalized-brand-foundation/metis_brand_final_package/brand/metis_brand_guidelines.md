
# Métis Brand Guidelines (Finalized)

## Brand Foundation
Métis is an intelligence analysis platform named after the Greek goddess of wisdom.
The brand represents strategic intelligence emerging from complex information environments.

Tone:
- Authoritative
- Strategic
- Discreet
- Premium

## Color System

### Wisdom Golds
- Deep Gold #7A5A12
- Antique Gold #A87C1F
- Wisdom Gold #D4A73A
- Radiant Gold #F2C14E
- Luminous Core #FFD977

### Signal Layer
- Magenta Pulse #FF2D8F
- Solar Orange #FF7A18
- Signal Yellow #FFD400
- Cyber Teal #32D1CC
- Neon Lime #A6FF00

Rules:
Gold represents confirmed intelligence.
Signal colors represent analytical findings.

## Typography
Authority Serif → titles and brand.
Modern Serif → UI, reports, body text.

## UI Philosophy
Dark environment surfaces with restrained color usage.
Signals highlight analytical meaning while gold indicates verified intelligence.

## Report System
Digital: dark cinematic reports.
Print: white minimal layouts with restrained gold accents.
